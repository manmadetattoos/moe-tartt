The Artist

Moe Tartt


Moe Tartt brings real power to The Grounded Art Collective. Before tattooing, he was a Golden Gloves boxing champion. He learned focus, grit, and discipline in the ring — and now he puts that same fire into every tattoo he makes.

He didn't lose the fighter mindset. He just switched from gloves to a tattoo machine.

Moe's style mixes American Traditional, Neo-Traditional, and New School. His work is bold, bright, and full of life. Thick lines. Strong colors. Big energy. His tattoos look like they have their own heartbeat.

He's inspired by anime and illustrative art, and he's always leveling up. Every piece feels fresh — part classic, part modern, all Moe.

"His books fill up fast because his work speaks for itself."
